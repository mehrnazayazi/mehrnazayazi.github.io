Interests

Professional

I am deeply passionate about research and aspire to continue my career in this field, engaging with both theoretical and practical problems .My fascination with theoretical challenges began early in life when I found joy in discovering patterns in numbers during primary school. This passion grew through my participation in math Olympiad classes in high school. Although I couldn’t always dedicate as much time as I wanted to these problems, the thrill of thinking through a complex issue for hours brought me immense satisfaction. Throughout my undergraduate studies, I consistently pursued math and theoretical courses, maintaining my love for theoretical work.

Growing up with an engineer father who preferred building solutions rather than consuming them, I developed a strong inclination for practical problem-solving. This blend of theoretical interest and hands-on problem-solving drives my approach: I thrive on the joy of finding solutions. I was initially drawn to computer graphics due to its rich use of mathematics, physics, and scientific computing. However, my internships at DreamWorks exposed me to another compelling aspect of this field—the intersection of computer science and art. This blend of technical and artistic elements captivated me even more.

As machine learning gained prominence, it piqued my curiosity as well. Working on a project that used convolutional neural networks (CNNs) to transfer styles to fluid simulations motivated me to explore machine learning further. I wanted to understand this field deeply and make informed decisions about its applications across various domains. If I were to summarize my interests, I would say I have a broad passion for theoretical computer science, with a particular fascination for interdisciplinary work, especially in fields intersecting with art and the humanities. I find it incredibly rewarding to apply computer science and quantitative methods in these areas, as they allow me to see the tangible impact of my work in meaningful and creative ways.
